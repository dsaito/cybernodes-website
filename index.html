Who we are

Our vision

Posted on Jan 10 2022

We are a selected team of cryto investors who believe in the life-changing opportunities promoted by cryptoassets. In this scenario, we have chosen the Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS) paradigm due to its decentralizing and eco-friendly fundaments, coupled with mutual gains to both delegators and validators. Aside from its time-tested rewards system, we believe that DPoS bolsters a marked sense of community, driving people towards a common objective: sustainable wealth and long-term financial freedom.

Our infrastructure

Posted on Jan 10 2022

Our infrastructure consists of nodes hosted on worldwide VPS providers, assuring blockchain decentralization, computing resources redundancy, and top-of-the-line security protocols. In addition, we run additional backup hardware to mitigate eventual downtimes due to server hangups and power outages. Our current hardware specs include 8-core vCPU, 32Gb RAM, 1Tb+ SSD node instances deployed on opposing global hemispheres, contributing to blockchain decentralization and DPoS projects security.
